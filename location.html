our location

Olympic Candy is a renowned candy company that is dedicated to providing high-quality and delicious candies to its customers. Our store is located in the heart of the bustling downtown area, surrounded by other popular shops and attractions. The building itself is an iconic landmark, featuring a striking facade and a charming interior that is filled with rows upon rows of colorful candies. When you step inside our store, you'll be greeted by the sweet aroma of freshly-made treats, and a friendly staff that is always happy to assist you with your candy needs. We take great pride in our store's appearance and the quality of our products, ensuring that every customer who walks through our doors has a delightful and memorable candy experience.
